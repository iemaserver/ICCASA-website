About ICCASA 2026

The 4th International Conference on Computational Applied Sciences and their Applications (ICCASA 2026) aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers, and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of Computational Sciences.

It also provides a premier interdisciplinary platform for researchers, practitioners, and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns as well as practical challenges encountered and solutions adopted in the fields of Applied Sciences and Engineering.

About UEM Jaipur

The University of Engineering & Management (UEM), Jaipur, is a premier university of North India with a legacy of excellence in technical education, research, and innovation.

UEM Jaipur has won the "Best University in North India" award and is renowned for its industry-oriented curriculum and high placement records. The campus vibrates with energy, fostering a culture of research, hackathons, and global collaboration.
