Publication Opportunities for ICCASA 2026

Publication Details

  1. All accepted papers, following their presentations, will have the opportunity to be published in the AIP Conference Proceedings (Indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, Inspec, Chemical Abstracts Service (CAS), and Astrophysics Data System (ADS)), in domains related to physics, chemistry, and mathematics, subject to the applicable article processing charges (APC).
  2. A limited number of selected research papers, following a double-blind peer review process, will have the opportunity to be published in Jangjeon Mathematical Society, a Scopus-Indexed Journal (at no cost), provided the work, in a domain related to mathematics.
  3. All the selected abstracts will be published in conference souvenir, a text book with valid ISBN.
